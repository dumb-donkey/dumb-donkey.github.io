WHO SHOULD PICK GBOARD?

If you rely on swipe typing, voice dictation or live translation every day, GBoard is the safer pick. Those are real strengths and TypeRight doesn't have them.

WHO SHOULD PICK TYPERIGHT?

Pick TypeRight if you want a keyboard that adapts to your thumbs instead of forcing your thumbs to adapt to it, if you love aesthetic phone setups (24 free animated skins), if you want typing to sound satisfying, if you're tired of losing copied text to a clipboard that wipes itself, or if you never want autocorrect changing your words behind your back again.

The short version: GBoard is the sensible sedan. TypeRight is the custom ride with glowing paint and a growl — built by one person, free, and genuinely different.
